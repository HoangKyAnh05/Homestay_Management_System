HUONG DAN CAU HINH SEPAY CHO HOMESTAY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
=======================================================

1. Mo tai khoan SePay va lien ket ngan hang
- Dang ky/dang nhap tai: https://my.sepay.vn
- Them tai khoan ngan hang se nhan tien thanh toan.
- Hoan tat cac buoc xac thuc ma SePay va ngan hang yeu cau.

2. Cau hinh ma thanh toan
He thong tao ma thanh toan theo dang:
HMS + ID payment

Vi du:
HMS15
HMS128

Tren dashboard SePay:
- Vao Cong ty -> Cau hinh chung -> Cau truc ma thanh toan.
- Bat Nhan dien ma thanh toan.
- Tao mau:
  Tien to: HMS
  Loai hau to: So nguyen
  Do dai toi thieu: 1
  Do dai toi da: 18

SEPAY_PAYMENT_CODE_PREFIX trong backend phai trung chinh xac voi tien to da
cau hinh tren SePay.

3. Tao webhook
Tren dashboard SePay:
- Vao Webhooks -> Them webhook.
- URL:
  https://TEN-MIEN-BACKEND/api/payments/sepay/webhook
- Loai su kien: Tien vao.
- Tai khoan: chon dung tai khoan khai bao trong SEPAY_ACCOUNT_NUMBER.
- Bat "Chi gui khi co ma thanh toan".
- Neu co bo loc tien to, chon HMS.
- Xac thuc: HMAC-SHA256.
- Copy Secret Key ngay khi SePay hien thi.

Webhook production phai la HTTPS va truy cap duoc tu Internet.
localhost:8080 khong the nhan webhook tu SePay.

Khi chay local, dung tunnel, vi du:
https://abc.example-tunnel.app/api/payments/sepay/webhook

4. Cau hinh application-local.properties
Tao file:
homestayManagement/src/main/resources/application-local.properties

Co the sao chep tu application-local.properties.example, sau do them:

SEPAY_BANK_NAME=Vietcombank
SEPAY_ACCOUNT_NUMBER=0123456789
SEPAY_ACCOUNT_HOLDER=NGUYEN VAN A
SEPAY_PAYMENT_CODE_PREFIX=HMS
SEPAY_TRANSFER_PREFIX=
SEPAY_WEBHOOK_SECRET=secret_hmac_sao_chep_tu_sepay

Y nghia:
- SEPAY_BANK_NAME:
  Ten ngan hang theo ten SePay/VietQR ho tro, vi du Vietcombank, BIDV,
  MBBank, ACB, TPBank.
- SEPAY_ACCOUNT_NUMBER:
  So tai khoan nhan tien. Phai trung voi tai khoan cua webhook.
- SEPAY_ACCOUNT_HOLDER:
  Ten chu tai khoan hien thi tren man hinh thanh toan.
- SEPAY_PAYMENT_CODE_PREFIX:
  Tien to ma thanh toan. Mac dinh HMS.
- SEPAY_TRANSFER_PREFIX:
  Chuoi bat buoc dat truoc ma thanh toan neu ngan hang/VA yeu cau.
- SEPAY_WEBHOOK_SECRET:
  Secret cua webhook HMAC-SHA256. Khong commit gia tri nay len Git.

5. Cau hinh theo loai ngan hang

Tai khoan thong thuong:
SEPAY_TRANSFER_PREFIX=

VietinBank ca nhan/ho kinh doanh:
Tai lieu SePay yeu cau noi dung VietQR co chuoi SEVQR.
Dung:
SEPAY_TRANSFER_PREFIX=SEVQR

Ket qua noi dung chuyen khoan se co dang:
SEVQRHMS15

VA theo noi dung chuyen khoan:
Neu ma VA la 001, SePay yeu cau tien to TKP001.
Dung:
SEPAY_TRANSFER_PREFIX=TKP001

Ket qua:
TKP001HMS15

VA chinh thuc:
SEPAY_ACCOUNT_NUMBER phai la so VA dung de tao VietQR, khong phai so tai
khoan goc.

6. Bao mat webhook
Backend thuc hien:
- Doc raw request body.
- Kiem tra X-SePay-Timestamp khong qua 5 phut.
- Xac minh X-SePay-Signature bang HMAC-SHA256.
- Chi chap nhan giao dich transferType=in.
- Kiem tra dung so tai khoan nhan.
- Kiem tra ma thanh toan va so tien.
- Chong xu ly trung bang SePay transaction ID unique.

Khong tat HMAC tren production.

7. Luong thanh toan
- Booking can thanh toan co trang thai PENDING.
- Khach bam Thanh toan.
- He thong tao Invoice va Payment PENDING.
- Frontend hien QR SePay, so tien va noi dung chuyen khoan.
- Khach quet QR va chuyen khoan.
- SePay gui webhook giao dich tien vao.
- Backend xac minh webhook va doi chieu giao dich.
- Payment chuyen SUCCESS.
- Booking va BookingDetail chuyen CONFIRMED.
- Hoa don va giao dich duoc luu trong database.

8. Test mode
- SePay co Test mode rieng biet voi Live.
- Tao tai khoan, mau ma thanh toan va webhook trong Test mode.
- Mo phong giao dich co noi dung HMS<ID_PAYMENT>.
- Kiem tra response webhook phai la:
  HTTP 200
  {"success":true}

9. Kiem tra truoc khi chay
- MySQL dang chay.
- Backend cong 8080.
- Frontend cong 5173.
- Tai khoan ngan hang da lien ket SePay.
- Mau ma thanh toan HMS da bat.
- Webhook HMAC da tao va URL truy cap duoc.
- SEPAY_ACCOUNT_NUMBER trung voi tai khoan webhook.

10. Migration hoa don thanh toan online
Hoa don SePay duoc tao tu dong nen khong co nhan vien lap hoa don.
Ung dung se tu dong chuyen invoices.employee_id sang nullable khi khoi dong.

Neu tai khoan MySQL production khong co quyen ALTER TABLE, DBA can chay mot lan:

ALTER TABLE invoices
MODIFY COLUMN employee_id BIGINT NULL;
